Transforming Literacy for Children with Dyslexia

SparkLexia is a 2D educational mobile game designed specifically to help children with dyslexia improve their reading, spelling, and phonemic awareness through engaging, multisensory gameplay.

Why SparkLexia Works

Our evidence-based approach combines multisensory learning with engaging gameplay to address the unique challenges faced by children with dyslexia.

Engaging Gameplay Experience

SparkLexia offers a variety of interactive activities designed to make learning enjoyable and effective.

Interactive Learning Modules

Our game includes multiple engaging activities:

  • Letter-Sound Matching Games
  • Interactive Storytelling with Choices
  • Emotion-Driven Story Decisions
  • Word Tracing and Construction
  • Matching Image Activities
  • Word Puzzles and Spelling Challenges

Each activity is designed with dyslexia-friendly fonts, clear audio instructions, and visual cues to minimize cognitive overload.
